About Us

Forging Champions Since 2010

Elite martial arts training that transforms body, mind and spirit.

Uyir Kalai National Sports Academy is dedicated to preserving and promoting India's rich martial arts heritage. We offer world-class training in six traditional disciplines — developing not just athletic ability, but warrior character.

Our coaches are nationally certified, and our students have won medals at district, state, national and international levels. We believe every student has a champion within — our mission is to bring it out.

Meet our Founder
UYIR KALAI THALAIVAN
Founder & Chief Master — Uyir Kalai National Sports Academy

With over 15+ years of dedicated service to India's martial arts heritage, our Founder & Chief Master has shaped hundreds of warriors across Chennai and beyond. A passionate champion of traditional combat arts — Silambam, Adimurai, Thang-Ta & Gatka — he has led students to victories at District, State, National and International levels.

His philosophy is simple: "Train the body, sharpen the mind, forge the spirit." Every student who walks through our doors is transformed into a disciplined, confident champion.

Questions?

Common Inquiries

Clear answers to help you start your journey

What is the minimum age to join?
+
We welcome students from age 5 and above. We have special batches designed specifically for kids, youth, and adults to ensure age-appropriate training.
Do I need any prior experience?
+
Not at all! Most of our students start as complete beginners. Our masters will guide you from the very fundamentals to advanced levels at your own pace.
What are the class timings?
+
We have flexible morning and evening sessions. Morning: 6AM - 8AM. Evening: 5PM - 8PM. Weekend special batches are also available at our Minjur branch.
Is there a certificate after completion?
+
Yes! We provide certificates for level completions and belt upgrades. Our certificates are recognized by JASK and SGFI, helping students in sports quota admissions.
